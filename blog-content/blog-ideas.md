The Core SEO Strategy

Think of Helmful as:

"HubSpot for boat dealerships"

Your content should answer:

How do I sell more boats?
How do I manage leads better?
How do I improve follow-up?
How do I reduce lost opportunities?
How do I modernize dealership operations?
How do I market my dealership?

Those are the searches that can eventually convert into demos.

Company News

Keep this minimal.

Maybe:

Product launches
New integrations
Customer success stories
Event attendance
Partnership announcements

I would not spend more than 10% of publishing capacity here.

Product Updates

Don't write release notes.

Turn them into business outcomes.

Bad:

Helmful Adds Lead Assignment Feature

Better:

How Automated Lead Assignment Helps Boat Dealers Respond Faster

Bad:

New Reporting Dashboard Released

Better:

The 5 Metrics Every Boat Dealer Should Track Weekly
